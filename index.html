Hi, I'm Neel Belsare, an AI & IoT enthusiast passionate about building futuristic projects that merge automation and intelligence. I specialize in AI, IoT, Robotics, and Full-Stack Development, constantly exploring innovative solutions to real-world problems. As a B.Tech student in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science at Jawaharlal Nehru Engineering College, MGMU, I collaborate with like-minded peers, to push the boundaries of technology.
